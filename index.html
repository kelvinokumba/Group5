THE BIG FIVE

In Africa, the Big Five game animals are the Lion, Leopard, Rhinoceros, Elephant, and Buffalo. They are among the most famous of Africa's large animals. The term was coined by big-game hunters, and refers to the five most difficult animals in Africa to hunt on foot but is now more widely used by game viewing tourists and safari tour operators.
